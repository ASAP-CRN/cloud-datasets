# "iSCORE-PD: NGS genotyping and zygosity testing of isogenic hESC clones".

## Dataset Description:

Targeted DNA sequencing data for the iSCORE-PD isogenic hESC clone collection, encompassing three complementary analyses: (1) NGS genotyping of 53 mutant clones across 11 PD-linked genes via amplicon sequencing on Illumina iSeq 100 with CRISPResso2 analysis, (2) zygosity testing of a subset of clones in 6 genes via SNP-PCR (NGS or Sanger) to assess zygosity near CRISPR edit sites, and (3) Sanger sequencing of the wild-type allele in 5 DJ1/PARK7 heterozygous clones.
