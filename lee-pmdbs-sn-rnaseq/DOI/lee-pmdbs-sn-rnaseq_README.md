# "Single nucleus RNA-sequencing of human postmortem hippocampus, middle frontal gyrus, and substantia nigra".

## Dataset Description:
 
Single nucleus RNA-seq data from hippocampus, middle frontal gyrus, and substantia nigra samples of human PD and control postmortem brains. All three brain regions were co-sampled from each donor to enable direct comparison of transcriptomic signatures across anatomically and functionally distinct regions within the same brain.
